Meet Our Team

The passionate astrophotographers behind Ashtronomy

Our Story

We are a dedicated team of three astrophotographers who share a deep passion for capturing the wonders of the cosmos. Each of us brings unique skills and perspectives to our collaborative journey through the universe.

Ashwin

Primary Astrophotographer, Post-Processing, & Web Development

I'm Ashwin Krishnamurthy, an aspiring aerospace engineer with a deep passion for astrophotography. My journey began as an obsession with the night sky and its boundless mysteries, which gradually grew into a serious interest that I devoted more and more time to. Joining an observatory was a major step forward, as it allowed me to connect with professionals and use industry-grade equipment to further develop my skills. Beyond astronomy, I also love mathematics and physics, having participated in numerous projects and placed in several competitions.

Equipment I Operate:

  • Orion Starblast II 4.5 Eq - My very first telescope (reflector)
  • Observatory 26" Telescope - Professional-grade Newtonian reflector
  • Seestar - Smart automated telescope system

Pranesh

Primary Astrophotographer & Cameraman

Hey, I’m Pranesh Kirubakar. I spend a lot of late nights chasing clear skies with my Celestron StarExplorer, trying to capture just a fraction of the beauty that’s out there. For me, astrophotography isn’t just about the gear or the pictures — it’s about those quiet moments where the universe feels closer and more alive. What started as a small hobby turned into a real passion for learning how to frame galaxies, nebulae, and planets in ways that tell their own story. I’m always looking forward to the next night under the stars and sharing what I find along the way.

Equipment I Operate:

  • Celestron StarExplorer - My primary telescope (refractor)
  • Observatory 26" Telescope - Professional-grade Newtonian reflector
  • Canon Rebel T8I DSLR Camera - For capturing long exposures of the sky

Ashwath

Astrophotographer In-Training & Seestar Specialist

I'm Ashwath Krishnamurthy, a rising high schooler eager to learn more about astronomy. Watching my brother and his friends spend countless nights under the stars inspired me to get involved as well. I am a member of the Civil Air Patrol, which has sparked my interest in the world above, and now I hope to use telescopes to explore it further.

Equipment I Operate:

  • Orion Starblast II 4.5 Eq - Great beginner's telescope
  • SeeStar S50 - Smart automated telescope system
