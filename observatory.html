Welcome to My Cosmic Workshop

My observatory is more than just a place to observe the stars - it's my sanctuary, my laboratory, and my gateway to the universe. Built with passion and precision, every piece of equipment has been carefully chosen to capture the beauty of deep space objects.

Located in a dark sky area with minimal light pollution, the observatory provides the perfect conditions for both visual observation and astrophotography. The automated dome and computerized mount allow me to capture hours of data on a single target, revealing details invisible to the naked eye.

Equipment & Setup

Main Telescope

16" f/4.5 Newtonian Reflector

A custom-built Newtonian reflector with exceptional light-gathering capability. The large aperture allows for detailed views of deep space objects and excellent resolution for planetary imaging.

  • Aperture: 406mm (16")
  • Focal Length: 1827mm
  • Focal Ratio: f/4.5
  • Mount: Paramount ME II

Imaging Cameras

ZWO ASI2600MM Pro & ASI174MM Mini

Dedicated astronomy cameras for capturing deep space objects and planetary details. The cooled sensor reduces noise for long-exposure imaging, while the high-speed planetary camera captures fleeting moments of atmospheric stability.

  • Main Camera: ZWO ASI2600MM Pro
  • Sensor: APS-C CMOS
  • Cooling: -45Â°C below ambient
  • Planetary: ZWO ASI174MM Mini

Filters & Accessories

Chroma & Baader Filter Sets

High-quality narrowband and broadband filters for capturing specific wavelengths of light. This allows for detailed imaging of emission nebulae and planetary features.

  • Narrowband: Ha, OIII, SII
  • Broadband: LRGB
  • Planetary: IR, UV, Color
  • Focuser: MoonLite CRL 2.5"

Guiding System

Off-Axis Guider with ASI290MM

Precision guiding system that tracks stars to maintain perfect alignment during long exposures. Essential for capturing sharp, detailed images of deep space objects.

  • Guide Camera: ZWO ASI290MM
  • Guide Scope: 60mm f/4
  • Software: PHD2 Guiding
  • Accuracy: <0.5 arcsec RMS

Software & Processing

Acquisition Software

Sequence Generator Pro for automated imaging sequences, NINA for advanced camera control, and SharpCap for planetary imaging.

Processing Software

PixInsight for advanced image processing, Photoshop for final touches, and specialized tools for noise reduction and detail enhancement.

Analysis Tools

AstroImageJ for photometry, Astrometry.net for plate solving, and custom scripts for automated data analysis and quality assessment.
