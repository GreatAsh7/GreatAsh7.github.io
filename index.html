About Me

Welcome to my cosmic corner of the internet! I'm Ashwin, a passionate astrophotographer dedicated to capturing the beauty of our universe. Through long nights under the stars, I've learned to translate the wonders of space into images that tell stories of our solar system, DSOs, and other celestial events.

My journey in astronomy began with a simple telescope (Orion Starblast II 4.5) and has evolved into a deep exploration of both the technical and artistic aspects of astrophotography. Join me as I share my experiences and move forward in my astronomical journey!
