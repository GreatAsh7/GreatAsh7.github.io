June 11th, 2025 Astronomical Event

Strawberry Moon

Although it’s called the Strawberry Moon, it usually doesn’t appear red — or does it? This year, the Moon actually took on a subtle reddish hue, drawing crowds outside to catch a glimpse. I quickly set up my telescope to help them get a closer look.

Duration: 1 Hour
Location: My House
Guests: 20 People
April 8th, 2024 Astronomical Event

Solar Eclipse Viewing at School

To celebrate the partial solar eclipse, my school provided everyone with glasses and took us to the field for a viewing session. I watched demonstrations performed by my teachers, made new astro buddies, and got some nice pictures!

Duration: 2 Hours
Location: My School, Edison, NJ
Guests: 150+ Students
